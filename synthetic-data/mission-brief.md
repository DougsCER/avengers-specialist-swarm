## Situation

A coordinated, multi-vector global crisis is unfolding simultaneously across three domains:

**1. Climate Tipping Point**
Arctic methane release has crossed a threshold not seen in 800,000 years. Current models project a 4.5°C temperature rise within 30 years if no intervention. Coastal cities housing 1.1 billion people face inundation. Agricultural collapse in equatorial regions is projected within 15 years, threatening food security for 3 billion people.

**2. Emerging Pathogen**
A novel zoonotic pathogen — designation: VENOM-7 — has emerged in three continents simultaneously. Transmission vectors are unclear. Current R0 estimate: 4.2. No existing vaccine. Standard containment protocols are failing. WHO projects 500M infections within 18 months without a breakthrough intervention.

**3. Infrastructure Collapse**
Critical global infrastructure — power grids, financial systems, logistics networks — is under coordinated cyberattack from a stateless threat actor with capabilities exceeding any known nation-state. Seven countries have experienced grid blackouts. The attack signature suggests AI-assisted autonomous malware with adaptive evasion.
